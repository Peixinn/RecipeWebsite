MAC AND CHEESE

INGREDIENTS

2.5 cups milk

2 bays leaves

1 lb elbow, shell, ziti,or similar pasta

4 tbsp (1/2 stick) butter

2 shallots (or 1/2 yellow onion), finely chopped

3 tbsp all-purpose flour

1.5 cups grated sharp cheddar or gouda

1.5 cups grated Parmesan cheese

1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs

Freshly-ground black pepper

Salt to taste

STEPS

1.Preheat the oven to 400, bring a large pot of salted water to boil

2.Grease a roasting pan with 1 tbsp butter

3.In a frying pan, fry the shallots and garlic until golden brown

4.Cook the milk with bay leaves in a small saucepan over medium-low heat.

5.When small bubbles appear along the sides (about 5 mins) turn off the heat, add the cooked shallots and garlic, and set aside

6.Cook the pasta until it is nearly done, drain and rinse to stop cooking

7.In a saucespan over medium-low heat,melt 3 tbsp butter

8.When it's foamy add flour and cook,stirring until the mixture browns(about 5 mins)

9.Remove bay leaves from milk and add,1/4 cup at a time,into the flour mixture,stirring with whisk constantly

10.As soon as mixture becomes smooth,add more milk until the mixture is completely smooth

11.Add the cheddar/gouda and stir

12.Dump the pasta into a roasting pan and pour the cheese mix on top, top with parmesan ans salt and pepper,stir

13.Top with breadcrumbs and bake until bubbling,about 15 mins

NOTES

  • Optional:add smoked paprika at the end with the salt and pepper
  • As per the amazing ribs recipe,instead of baking in the oven
  • A different take (and with the bacon and jalepenos!)
